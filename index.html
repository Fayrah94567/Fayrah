About Me

My name is Fayrah Li, a senior student with the title of an aspiring student. I am working toward a double major in Psychology and Digital New Media. I hold a strong curiosity around human thought patterns and behaviors, alongside the influence social media and digital platforms exert on daily human life. This dual major lets me study human psychology and contemporary digital communication from two complementary viewpoints. I love ice skating during my free time as a way to relax and stay focused.

While I am still exploring my long-term career path, I stay eager to absorb new knowledge, explore new chances, and eventually build a career that perfectly merges my academic and personal interests.

Skills & Hobbies

Working through intricate formulas and multi-layered math problems not only brought me academic success, but also trained me to untangle complex concepts, analyze problems from multiple viewpoints, and deliver structured, stable solutions. These creative hobbies have refined my observational skills and personal aesthetic taste, balancing the rigid logic I built from mathematics and granting me more flexible, creative problem-solving perspectives.
